Common questions

How do I find someone to subscribe to?

Tap No on the main screen, then tap the profile icon in the top right. From there you can search for someone by their display name and send them a subscribe request.

How do I approve a subscriber?

Go to your profile screen. Any pending subscription requests will appear there with approve and decline buttons.

Why can I only send one message per day?

Tible is designed around a single daily message — one intentional thing to share with the people who follow you. The one-per-day limit is core to what makes Tible different.

How do I share a message I received?

When you receive a push notification from someone you subscribe to, swipe down on the notification to reveal a Share button. Tapping it will let you share the message via iMessage or any other app.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the profile screen and tap "delete account" next to the sign out button. This will permanently delete your account, all your messages, and all your subscriptions. This action cannot be undone.

How do I reset my password?

On the log in screen, tap "forgot password?" and enter your email address. We'll send you a reset link.
